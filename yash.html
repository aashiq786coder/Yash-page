YASH

Yash is a popular Indian actor, born Naveen Kumar Gowda on January 8, 1986, in Hassan, Karnataka, who is best known for his role as Rocky in the K.G.F. franchise. He is married to actress Radhika Pandit, with whom he has two children, and has an estimated height of 5′ 10¾″ (1.80 m). Yash began his career in television and theatre before making his film debut in 2007, and has since become one of the highest-paid actors in the Kannada film industry.
